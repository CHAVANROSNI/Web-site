ABOUT ACM

ACM, the Association for Computing Machinery, is the world's largest computing society, founded in 1947. It fosters collaboration among educators, researchers, and practitioners, promoting advancements in computing through publications, conferences, and Special Interest Groups (SIGs). ACM plays a crucial role in advancing computing as both a science and profession, supporting global networking, knowledge sharing, and professional development.
