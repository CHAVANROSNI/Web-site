CHAPTERS

ACM membership offers resources to help you shape the future – with lifelong learning programs, publications providing original research and practical content, special interest groups and local chapters to meet peers, delve deeper into technical areas, and leadership opportunities on stimulating projects. The world’s leading computing professionals choose the world’s leading computing society because it helps them be their most creative, keep abreast of emerging trends and technologies, stay connected to their colleagues and make a positive impact on the profession and the world at large.

  1. The opportunity to join one or more of ACM's Special Interest Groups in 37 specialty areas of IT.
  2. A vast network of nearly 100,000 highly dedicated student and professional peers.
  3. Become a member of the computing community through one of hundreds of Professional and Student Chapters worldwide.
  4. Unique volunteering opportunities to gain hands-on experience and knowledge of the marketplace.
  5. Student Quick Takes, ACM's quarterly newsletter for students
